About Us

WHO WE ARE:

Incepted in the year 2014, Dr. Realty nestled under the valued and trusted Vrifid Homes LLP envisions to be a one-stop real estate solution in India. Bringing years of trust with a futuristic and modern approach, the brand holds a unique outlook towards real estate services making it a transparent, hassle-free, and smooth process for all its clients and associates.

Founded on the values of commitment, excellence, and customer satisfaction, Dr. Realty under the leadership of its Founding Chairman Ram Kumar Gupta benefits greatly from his experience, expertise, and knowledge of real estate and its associated services in India.

Based out of Noida, Uttar Pradesh, the brand has established milestones in its journey by redefining the real estate service and processes. It has successfully associated with industry leaders Godrej Properties, Omaxe, DLF, Wave City, Bhutani, I-Thum World, Spectrum Metro, Eldeco, Gaurs Group, Saya and many more, effectively providing them with diverse services in the real estate segment.

Dr. Realty standing true to its motto ‘We Don’t Follow the Trend, We Honor your Trust’ is currently serving the most notable brands in India including Amazon, Cinepolis, Aditya Birla Fashion Retail, Manayvar, Just in Time, Metro Shoes, Maruti Suzuki, etc.
With its Head Office in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, it is ambitiously expanding, marking its presence in metropolitans and corporate capitals like Gurgaon, Noida, and Lucknow. It is thriving and leading the real estate service segment by delivering quality services of Construction, Renovation, Interiors, Consultancy, Acquisition, Architecture & Design, Franchising & Licensing, and Purchase & Sale of industrial, commercials, and residential properties.
Amalgamating entrepreneurship with social commitment, Dr. Realty is unarguably innovating real estate in India by building a trustworthy and client-centric service sector making it the leader in real estate and its associated services.
