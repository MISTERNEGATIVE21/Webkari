Escon Plots & Villas is a premium integrated township located at Sector 150 Noida. Escon Plots & Villas Sector 150 modern-day Residential Project provides all modern amenities which are necessary to maintain a high standard of living and is a low density project. Escon Plots & Villas offers luxury villas in Noida configurations of 4 BHK Villas & 5 BHK Villas in Noida Sector 150.

Great planning & design is just the beginning of a better life at Escon Plots & Villas. Here you’ll find the perfect balance of green living with an active, urban lifestyle. Well-designed clusters comprising stately villas are anchored around an expanse of Shaheed Bhagad Singh Park. This premium township comes fitted with not just the privileges you need, but even those you still haven’t thought about.

Poised for immense commercial growth, with well-planned residential layouts, wide roads, faster connectivity via the Noida-Greater Noida Expressway and the upcoming metro, some of the best international schools and fully-equipped hospitals.

Location Highlights

Escon Plots & Villas is located in a pricey neighbourhood at Noida Sector-150 (Sports City), Numerous recreational facilities are available in the sector promoting an active and balanced living.


  • It is the greenest sector of Noida with about 80 percent of the green cover area.
  • Shaheed Bhagat Singh park spread over 40 acres, next to our project.
  • Sector - 150 is a low density sector, thus each & every family can enjoy more benefit of resources.
  • Easily accessible location 6-Lane Noida- Greater Noida Expressway.
  • Location is near the upcoming FNG (Faridabad- Noida- Ghaziabad) Expressway.
  • Easy connectivity to rest of NCR via Noida Greater Noida Metro.
  • Nearest metro station - Noida Sector - 148 on Aqua line.

Park

Just Next to Shaheed Bhagat Singh Park

Shaheed Bhagat Singh Park, located next to Noida – Greater Noida Expressway in Sector 150, was constructed in the memory of Country’s Great Martyr Bhagat Singh to honour his sacrifice and struggle for the freedom of India. Spread across the area of 40 acres, Bhagat Singh Park is the biggest park in Sector 150.

With waterside eateries, one can witness beautiful landscape, pleasure garden (amod vatika), children’s garden (baal vatika), garden of fragrance (sugnadh vatika), palm garden, and bamboo garden (baans vatika) with functional components such as pavilions, patios, resting places, and shrub beds.
