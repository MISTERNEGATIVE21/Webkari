Delivery Policy: Escon Infra Realtor

Thank you for choosing Escon Infra Realtor Private Limited for your property needs. Our delivery policy is designed to ensure a smooth and transparent process for the delivery of properties to our valued customers. Please read the following terms carefully:


1. Delivery Timeline: Upon completion of the property and receipt of full payment or adherence to agreed-upon payment milestones, we will initiate the delivery process. The specific delivery timeline will be communicated to you during the purchase process and may vary depending on factors such as property type, location, and any customizations.



2. Inspection Prior to Delivery: Before the delivery of the property, we encourage buyers to conduct a thorough inspection to ensure that the property meets their expectations and adheres to the agreed-upon specifications. Any discrepancies or issues should be reported to us promptly for resolution.



3. Delivery Process: Our team will coordinate with you to schedule the delivery of the property at a mutually convenient time. During the delivery process, our representatives will be available to address any questions or concerns you may have.



4. Documentation: Upon delivery of the property, you will be provided with all necessary documentation, including but not limited to the title deed, occupancy certificate (if applicable), and any warranties or guarantees associated with the property.



5. Handover Procedure: A formal handover of the property will be conducted to ensure that both parties acknowledge the successful delivery. This may include signing off on delivery documents and inspecting the property together to confirm its condition.



6. Post-Delivery Support: Our commitment to customer satisfaction extends beyond the delivery of the property. Should you encounter any issues or require assistance after taking possession, our customer support team will be readily available to assist you.



7. Cancellation or Rescheduling: In the event that you need to cancel or reschedule the delivery of the property, please notify us as soon as possible. Depending on the circumstances, cancellation fees or rescheduling charges may apply.



8. Force Majeure: While we strive to adhere to the agreed-upon delivery timeline, unforeseen circumstances such as natural disasters, labor strikes, or government regulations may impact our ability to deliver the property on time. In such cases, we will make every effort to mitigate the delay and keep you informed of any changes to the delivery schedule.



9. Contact Information: For any inquiries or assistance regarding the delivery of your property, please contact our customer service team at Phone Number: +91-7669 222 555or Email: support@esconinfrarealtor.com

By proceeding with the purchase of a property from Escon Infra Realtor Private Limited, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to our delivery policy outlined above.

Escon Infra Realtor Private Limited reserves the right to update or modify this delivery policy at any time without prior notice.
