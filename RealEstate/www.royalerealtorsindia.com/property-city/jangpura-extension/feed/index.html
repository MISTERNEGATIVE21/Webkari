An address that’s hard to forget, the condominium residences come serviced by the unmatched hospitality of Trident Hotels. You’ll find a level of comfort and elegance balanced with an equal measure of warmth, familiarity, and nostalgia – like that feeling you get when you’re at home. Residents will enjoy exclusive privileges and premium experiences curated by Trident Hotels, along with a mix of well-appointed amenities such as a state-of-the-art fitness center, resident lounge, children’s play area, landscaped gardens, landscaped rooftop lounge with screen and seating area, 24 hours security with access control, centralized maintenance, and fire & line safety systems.

Trident Residences is a prized possession for those who appreciate beauty, luxury, and the best of Delhi city. The development sits in a location surrounded by green spaces that are within walking distance of New Delhi’s major locations. The stunning views from its buildings overlook Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium, Khan Market, and Lodhi Gardens among many other iconic landmarks.

INTUITIVE DESIGN

An extension of their green surroundings, Trident Residences are designed as pedestrianized realms and raised the ground on V-shaped pilotis. This allows residents to move seamlessly through the property’s beautiful landscapes. A pedestrian gate allows residents and guests to enter from the street. Vehicles are directed to an automated car park in the basement, with access from the rear of the site. The building’s architectural language has a distinctive elegance and contemporary minimalism of urban living.

THE LANDSCAPE

The landscaped grounds preserve aged trees inherited with the site, which add a natural palette that celebrates seasonal variations. The perimeter walls of the ground floor are lined with vertical planters to complement the site’s landscape, purifying the air around its wooden seating decks and pedestrian pathways.

THE INTERIORS

The neutral yet distinct treatment of the interiors provides the ideal setting for residents to personalise their homes with their own furniture, objets d’art, and artefacts. Each room receives ample natural light and ventilation. The bedrooms, bathrooms, family lounge, kitchen and living room frame uninterrupted views of the stadium’s landscaped gardens while ensuring privacy for its residents.

DEVELOPER

Trident Residences has been conceptualized by BI Luxury residences, a pillar amidst the BI group of companies. Founded in the year 1935, the group spans businesses across hospitality, real estate, education, and media. Exuding an unwavering commitment to excellence, the BI group has established award-winning properties in India and across the globe in segments including heritage estates and 4-5 star establishments, the most recent opening being the refurbishment and relaunch of the stunning 16th century Billesley Manor Hotel in England. The BI Group has also been instrumental in the redevelopment of several commercial buildings residing in Delhi, occupied by renowned Indian and multinational companies alike. Its formidable real estate portfolio encompasses prime land parcels acquired to develop high-end luxury residences in Delhi, Bengaluru, and Goa, all of which are undergoing different stages of development.

OPERATOR

Trident Residences will be operated and serviced by Trident Hotels. Trident Hotels is a chain of 5-star hotels under the aegis of The Oberoi Group of Hotels: pioneers of luxury hospitality, serving guests with utmost love and care for over 85 years. Trident Hotels’ luxurious hospitality underpins the Group’s uncompromising standards and service quality. The signature Trident Hotels’ hospitality is the hallmark of Trident Residences. Founded in 1934, The Oberoi Group has a presence in seven countries under the Oberoi and Trident brand. It operates 33 hotels, Nile cruisers and a motor vessel in the backwaters of Kerala.

ARCHITECTS & DESIGNERS

Trident Residences has been designed by Architecture Discipline, a celebrated architectural firm that also conceived the luxurious suites at The Oberoi, New Delhi, Bellevue restaurant at The Oberoi, Amarvilas in Agra, and the Chairman’s Office at EIH Limited Headquarters in Gurgaon. The firm has received international recognition for its pioneering work, and has been cited among the most influential architects and interior designers in India. Its work is part of the India Pavilion at the London Design Biennale, representing the country on an international stage. The lighting design and planning of Trident Residences has been executed by Lirio Lopez, who is known for balancing naturally lit spaces with dynamic expressions of artificial lighting. I Design, the creative brain behind the community’s landscaping, weaves greenery into a seamlessly integrated design scheme, transforming the urban residence into a verdant oasis.
